Our Partnership

Globel InfoTech stands as a pioneering application platform tailored for Student Service Partners dedicated to facilitating international education endeavors. With its innovative features and seamless interface, Globel InfoTech empowers partners to navigate the complex admission process for students aspiring to study abroad.

In essence, Globel InfoTech revolutionizes the landscape of international education support by offering a robust and user-friendly platform that empowers Student Service Partners to navigate the complexities of the admission process with ease, ultimately contributing to the realization of students' aspirations to study abroad.

Here are five key aspects that underscore its effectiveness

The Globel InfoTech team will shortlist and contact eligible partners in their respective regions via telephonic calls or emails.

The Compliance team will check the eligibility criteria and verify the documents provided by you.

The Partnership team will send an MOU document for your digital signature.

Upon signature completion, a dedicated Account Manager and service team will be assigned to you.

Your Account Manager will welcome you with Admission portal login credentials and training documents.

The Globel InfoTech team will schedule a training session with all stakeholders as per your team’s availability.

Post successful training and onboarding, you can start processing your applications in the portal.

You can reach your Account Manager and service team for all day-to-day support.
